These rules are designed to help our community and keep everyone safe. Failure to read them is NOT AN EXCUSE and will most likely result in punishment.

1: Core

1.1: Respect

Treat all players and staff with basic decency. Hate speech, slurs, personal attacks, or any form of targeted harassment is not tolerated — even if disguised as a joke or using emojis.

1.2: Discrimination

Any kind of racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or similar behavior is absolutely intolerable and will be punished severely.

1.3: Toxicity

Don’t provoke, troll, or ruin someone else’s experience. If you're here to cause drama or push boundaries, you're not welcome.

1.4: Common Sense

If you’re asking “Can I say this and technically not break the rules?” — you already know the answer.

2: Fair Play

2.1: Unfair Advantages

No hacked clients, macros, auto-clickers, unfair mods, or bug abuse. Unsure? Ask staff first.

2.2: Bugs/Glitches

Report bugs. Don’t abuse them. Simple.

2.3: PvP

No cross-teaming or targeting. Play fair or don’t play.

2.4: Alt Accounts

Alts are fine within reason. No punishment evasion.

3: Chat & Communication

  • 3.1: Keep it clean — no excessive swearing, sexual content, or edgy jokes.
  • 3.2: No spam of any kind — messages, emojis, copypasta, etc.
  • 3.3: Keep usernames, pics, and shared media appropriate.
  • 3.4: Stay on topic — use the right channels.
  • 3.5: No politics, religion, or drama bait.
  • 3.6: No impersonation — staff or otherwise.

4: Staff & Authority

  • 4.1: Respect staff decisions. If they say stop — stop.
  • 4.2: Use tickets for appeals. Don’t DM staff unless asked.
  • 4.3: Don’t mini-mod. Reporting is helpful — backseat modding isn’t.

5: External Content & Links

  • 5.1: No advertising without permission — Minecraft servers, streams, etc.
  • 5.2: Random/shady links = deletion and possible punishment.
  • 5.3: Don’t share personal info — yours or others.

6: Terms & Conditions

  • 6.1: Follow Discord ToS & Guidelines — or get banned by Discord.
  • 6.2: Follow Minecraft’s EULA. No account sales, RMT, etc.
  • 6.3: Local laws still apply — threats, scams, etc. will be reported.
  • 6.4: You must be 13+ to use Discord. No exceptions.

By using the Gato Network, you agree to these rules. They may be updated — it's your job to stay informed. “I didn’t know” isn’t an excuse. Stay safe, stay smart, and report rulebreakers.
